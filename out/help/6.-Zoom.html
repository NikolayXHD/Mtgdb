6. Zoom

result action
zoom card image left click

Context menu on zoomed card. Use menu to

  • edit or open in explorer the image
  • enable / disable card duplicates when cycling image variants

Card duplicates are cards with the same name as zoomed one. Basic lands always have duplicates in the same set. Many cards have duplicates in other sets

 right click
zoom out left click again
cycle card image variants mouse wheel when zoomed
show card back for flipped cards mouse wheel when zoomed
show meld pair / melded card mouse wheel when zoomed
